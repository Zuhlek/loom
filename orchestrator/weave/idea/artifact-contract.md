# Idea Artifact Contract

## `idea.md`

- Must exist.
- Must have front matter with `project` and `created`.
- Must contain: What we're building, Scope, Out of scope, Expected behavior, Constraints, Acceptance boundaries.
- Must make remaining ambiguity explicit or state none.

## `decisions.md`

- Required after the first branching question.
- Every `loom:question` marker has a matching `loom:answer-slot`.
- Question categories are named categories only.
- Active decisions have answered slots or are explicitly deferred.
